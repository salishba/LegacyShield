   Dynamic Detection:
     • Detects applicable frameworks based on industry/role/data classification
     • Calculates framework-specific SLAs

   Purpose: Batch prioritization with multi-factor scoring
   Scoring Components:
     • Severity (25% weight) - CVSS, exploitability, ransomware, PoC
     • Exploitability (20% weight) - EPSS, attack complexity, privileges, network
     • Business Impact (25% weight) - System role, data classification,
                                       compliance violations
     • System Density (15% weight) - Affected systems, critical infrastructure,
                                      DMZ deployment, network exposure
     • SLA Urgency (15% weight) - Time to deadline (breached/at_risk/soon/compliant)
   Output: Sorted batch with rankings, SLA status, effort estimates
   Scalability: Handles thousands of vulnerabilities efficiently

     • Adoption Rate Risk (15%) - Environment adoption percentage
     • Known Issues Risk (30%) - Critical/high/medium/low issue count
     • System Criticality Risk (15%) - Role, uptime, backup, services
     • Dependency Risk (10%) - Application/component compatibility
   Output: RollbackRiskAssessment with:
     • Overall risk score (0.0-1.0)
     • Risk level (CRITICAL/HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)
     • Rollback feasibility (true/false)
     • Estimated rollback time (minutes)
